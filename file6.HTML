Introduction

What is it?

  • Email software is a program that includes features and functionality for utilizing electronic mail.

  • Email software is a program designed to host, optimize, or secure digital communications for personal or corporate usage.

HISTORY

On October 29th 1969, the first message was sent from computer to computer on ARPANET. It was 1971 when Ray Tomlinson invented and developed electronic mail, as we know it today, by creating ARPANET's networked email system. It was the first system able to send mail between users on different hosts connected to ARPANET.

He sent this history-making email to a desktop that was present in his own room in order to check the efficiency of the software. He was the one who started using the “@” symbol while sending emails.

In 1976, Queen Elizabeth II sent the first email on ARPANET’s electronic mail program on her visit to the Royal Signals and Radar Establishment. She used the username HME2 (Her Majesty Elizabeth II) and was the first head to do so..

Types of Email Software

Email Marketing

  • Email marketing software refers to programs that optimize email in order to convey marketing messages.
  • Examples of email marketing communications include real-time and custom communications, brand newsletters, fundraisers, and more.

Email Security

  • Email security software is a set of programs designed to predict, prevent, detect, and respond to potential attacks while also managing access.
  • Top email security vendors include Proofpoint, Avanan, Mimecast, Barracuda, and Cisco.
  • From blocking or flagging phishing emails to offering indicators of identity in email, email security software works across network gateways, user behavior, and network systems to secure email communications.

Email Optimization

  • The most recent generation of email software is designed to improve performance and adapt to evolving email marketing solutions.
  • By providing features for multichannel marketers, it goes beyond the limits of typical email marketing software. Email marketers have flexibility, agility, and specializations to optimize their efforts thanks to features like previews, pre-send checklists, and additional data analytics.
  • Top email optimization vendors include Litmus, Email Monks, Email on Acid, Trendline, and Validity.
